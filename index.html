    Performance Fuel

    NUTRITION
    DECODED

    Hover each pillar to learn exactly how to fuel performance, recovery, and body composition.

    🥩
    PROTEIN FIRST
    1.6–2.2g per kg bodyweight. Lean meats, eggs, and whey are your foundation for muscle synthesis and fat loss.
    ⚡
    SMART CARBS
    Time complex carbs around training. Oats pre-workout, fruit post. Fuel performance without spiking fat storage.
    🥑
    HEALTHY FATS
    20–35% of daily calories. Avocado, olive oil, and nuts regulate hormones, reduce inflammation, and protect joints.
    💧
    HYDRATION
    35–45ml per kg per day. Even 2% dehydration drops performance by 20%. Drink before you feel thirsty.
